## From Mom
### Ingredients
* 1 1/4 cup hot water
* 1 cup cut dates
* 3/4 cup shortening
* 1 cup sugar
* 2 eggs
* 2 cups flour
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1 tablesoon unsweetened cocoa
