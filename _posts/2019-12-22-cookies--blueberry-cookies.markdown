## From Grandma
### Ingredients
* 1/2 cup margarine
* 1 egg well beaten
* 1/2 cup milk
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 grated lemon rind
* 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
* 2 1/2 cups flour
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1 cup blueberries


Cream shortening and sugar and egg, then rind. Add milk and dry ingredients - alternately. Fold in blueberries.


Bake 375F for 12-15 minutes.
