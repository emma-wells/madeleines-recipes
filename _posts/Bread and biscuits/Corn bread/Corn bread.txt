#Corn bread
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 3/4 teaspoon salt
* 2 cups flour
* 1 teaspoon baking powder
* 2 eggs
* 1 1/2 cups milk
* 1 cup cornmeal
* 1 tablespoon melted butter

<br>Mix sugar with beaten eggs. Sift flour, salt, baking powder and cornmeal together. Add butter and milk. Beat it quickly and bake in a large, buttered pan in a very hot oven.
