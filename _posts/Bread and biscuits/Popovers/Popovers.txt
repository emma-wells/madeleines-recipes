#Popovers
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 cup all-purpose flour
* 3 whole eggs
* 1 cup milk
* 1/4 teaspoon milk

<br>Thoroughly mix all ingredients in a blender. Pour mixture into greased muffin tins and bake at 400F for 20 minutes. Turn heat down to 300F and continue to bake for another 25 minutes. Will make about six.</br>
