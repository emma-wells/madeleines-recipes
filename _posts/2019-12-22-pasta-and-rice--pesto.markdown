## From Grandma
### Ingredients
* 2 cups basil
* 1/2 cup parsley
* 1/4 cup pinenuts or walnuts
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
* 1/2 cup olive oil
* 4 tablespoons parmesan cheese


Put first six ingredients in the blender, then slowly add cheese and a dash of nutmeg.
