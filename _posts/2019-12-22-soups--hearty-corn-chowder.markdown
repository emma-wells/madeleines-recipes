## From Grandma
### Ingredients
* 2 packages of sausage meat
* 2 quarts of milk
* 2 cups, 1 pound & 10 ounces cream-style corn
* 2 teaspoons salt
* 1/4 cup cornstarch (cornflour)
* 1/2 cup water
* 2 cups (1 pound) lima beans
