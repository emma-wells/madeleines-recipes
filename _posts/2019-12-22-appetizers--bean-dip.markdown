## From Grandma

Drain and rinse 1 large can of Progresso Kidney beans. Add:


*  2-3 tablespoons Miracle Whip
* 1/2 small jar tartar sauce
* 3 teaspoons white horseradish
* 1 teaspoon garlic powder
* pepper


Mix and chill.
