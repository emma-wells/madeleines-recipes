#Penne in cream sauce with sausage
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 tablespoon butter
* 1 tablespoon olive oil 
* 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
* 3 garlic cloves, minced
* 1 pound sausages, with casing removed
* 2/3 cup white wine
* 1- 14 1/2 cups diced peeled tomatoes
* 1 cup whipping cream
* 6 tablespoons chopped Italian parsley
* 1 pound penne pasta
* 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated

<br>Melt butter and oil, and saute onion and garlic. Add sausage and saute until cooked through. Drain off excess grease and add wine. Boil until amost dry and add tomatoes. Simmer 3 minutes. Add cream and cook until it thickens, about 5 minutes. Add parsley, salt and pepper.</br>
