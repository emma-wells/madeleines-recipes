#Tomato sauce
##from Grandma
###Inredients
* 2 pounds fresh or 2 cups canned tomatoes
* 1 medium onion
* 1/4 pound butter
* 1/4 teaspoon granulated sugar

<br>1. Wash tomatoes and cook for 10 minutes.</br><br>2. Puree tomatoes then pour back in pot. Add butter, onion, salt and sugar. Cook 45 minutes. Discard onion.</br>

<br>Emma's note: This is almost exactly Marcella Hazan's '4 ingredient' pasta sauce.</br>
