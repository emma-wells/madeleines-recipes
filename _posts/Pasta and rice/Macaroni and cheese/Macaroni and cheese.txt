#Macaroni and cheese
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 package elbow macaroni, cooked
* 1/4 cup butter
* 1/4 cup flour
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1/8 teaspoon pepper
* 2 cups milk
* 8 ounces cheddar cheese

<br>Melt butter, stir in flour, salt and pepper until smooth. Stir in milk and bring to a boil. Let sauce simmer 1 minute. Stir in 1 cup of cheese and macaroni. Pour into a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish. Bake 15-20 minutes at 375F.</br>
