#Pastitso
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 pound macaroni
* 1 pound hamburg
* 10-11 eggs
* Cinnamon
* Salt
* Pepper
* 1/4 pound butter
* 1 pound romano or Parmesan
* 1 quart milk

<br>Cook macaroni. Melt 1/8-pound butter in a frying pan and cook hamburg. Add salt, pepper and cinnamon.</br><br>Grease baking pan with butter. Sprinkle bottom with a generous amount of grated cheese. Then take well-drained macaroni and layer with hamburg alternately with cheese. Beat eggs and milk, then pour over macaroni. Sprinkle cinnamon on top and bake at 350F for 50-60 minutes.</br>
