#Fig squares
##From Grandma
###Crust
* 3 cups flour
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 2 teaspoons baking powder
* 2 teaspoons sugar
* 1/4 cup dry milk
* 1 1/4 cup shortening

<br>Add enough water to make it workable.</br>

###Filling
<br>Boil 2 cups sugar - 2 cups water for 5 minutes. Add 1 pound ground figs. Bake at 375-400F.</br>
