#Quiche Lorraine
##From Grandma
<br>Sift together:</br>

* 1 cup flour
* 1 teaspoon salt

<br>Cut in:</br>

* 1/3 cup shortening

<br>Sprinkle:</br>

* 3 to 4 teaspoons cold water

<br>Makes an 8" pie pan. (larger than intended).</br>

###Filling
<br>Fry 1/2 pound of bacon, and crumble into pie. Arrange 1/4 pound swiss cheese or American cheese over the bacon. Beat 3 eggs and 2 cups of milk, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon pepper and cayenne pepper. Blend and pour over bacon and cheese.Bake at 400F for 35-45 minutes.</br>
