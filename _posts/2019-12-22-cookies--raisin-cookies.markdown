## From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 2/3 cup butter
* 1/2 cup brown sugar
* 6 tablespoons ??? sugar
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 1 cup flour
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1 1/2 cup rolled oats
* 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
* 1 cup raisins


Combine butter, sugars, eggs and vanilla. Beat until smooth. Combine oats, flour, baking soda, and salt. Blend and stir in raisins.
