#Snowballs
##From Grandma
* 1 stick Oleo (margarine)
* 1 beaten egg
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 cup chopped dates (8 oz)(Dromedary, pitted)
<br>Cook for 10 minutes in pan. Cool.</br>
<br>Add:</br>
* 2 cup rice crispies
* 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
* 1/2 cup chopped nuts
<br>Mix. Form into balls. Roll in coconut.</br>
