#Coconut cake
##From Mom, and Posy Patch (chatter)
###Ingredients
* 3 cups sifted cake flour
* 3 teaspoons baking powder
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1 cup butter or shortening
* 1 cup finally chopped coconut
* 2 cups sugar
* 4 eggs
* 1 cup milk
* 1 teaspoon vanilla

<br>Sift dry ingredients. Cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs, 1 at a time, beat well after each 1. Add milk and vanilla alternately with dry ingredients to creamed mixture. Blend thoroughly.</br>
</br>Blend in coconut. Bake in 3 greased and lightly floured 9" round pans. Bake at 375F for 25-30 minutes.</br>
<br>Can be filled with rasberry jam between layers, then a fluffy white frosting. Final touch can be a topping with more shredded coconut.</br>
