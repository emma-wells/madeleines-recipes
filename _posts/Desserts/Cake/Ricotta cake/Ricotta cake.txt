#Ricotta cake
##From Grandma
<br>Use Duncan Hines Yellow Deluxe cake mix. Make as directed on package. Filling:</br>

* 1 large cup of ricotta (2 pounds)
* 4 eggs
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1 teaspoon vanilla

<br>Mix all together. Pour over batter that has been poured into a 15" long and 2" deep cake pane. Bake at 350F for 1 hour and 5 minutes</br>
