#Lemon tea cakes
##From Grandma ??
###Ingredients
* 1 1/2 teaspoon vinegar
* 1/2 cup milk
* 1/2 cup butter
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1 egg
* 1 teaspoon shredded lemon peel
* 1 3/4 cup sifted all-purpose flour
* 1 teaspoon baking powder
* 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/4 teaspoon salt

<br>Stir vinegar into milk. Cream butter and 3/4 cup sugar until fluffy. Add eggs and lemon peel; beat well. Sift together dry ingredients and add to butter mixture, alternatively with milk, beating until smooth after each addition. Drop from a teaspon 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350F 12-14 minutes. Brush at once with a lemon glaze.
###Lemon glaze
<br>Mix 3/4 cup sugar (Emma: Confectionary sugar?) with 1/4 cup lemon juice.</br>
