#Pineapple rightside up cake
##From Grandma
###Bake at 350F, 45 minutes
###Ingredients
* 2 cups flour
* 1/2 cup oil
* 2 eggs
* 1 1/2 cups sugar
* 1/2 cup chopped almonds
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1 20oz can of crushed pineapple in juice, drained

<br>Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Beat until smooth. Pour into a greased and floured 13x9 baking pan.</br>
