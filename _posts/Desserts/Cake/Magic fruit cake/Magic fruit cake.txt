#Magic fruit cake
##From Mom, via Pink Castle
###Ingredients
* 1 9-ounce package of fruit mince meat
* 1/2 cup water
* 1 can condensed milk
* 1 cup nuts, chopped
* 2 3-ounce candied fruits (3/4 cup)
* 1 egg 
* 3/4 cup flour
* 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

<br>Break mince meat into small pieces and put in a 2-quart saucepan. Add water. Boil briskly, 1 minute, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and cool. Add remaining ingredients in order given. Pour into 9x4x3 inch loaf pan, which has been greased and lined with greased waxed paper. Bake in oven for 1 1/2 hours.</br>
