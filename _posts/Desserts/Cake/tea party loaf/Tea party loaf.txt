#Tea party loaf
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 3 cups prepared biscuit mix
* 1 cup quick rolled oats
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 egg slightly beaten
* 1 1/2 cup milk
* 1/4 cup vegetable oil
* 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate pieces
* 1/2 cup butterscotch pieces
* 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
<br>Measure biscuit mix into bowl; stir in rolled oats and sugar. Combine egg, milk and oil; add to oat mixture; beat until smooth. Stir in chocolate and butterscotch pieces and nuts.</br>
<br>Turn into greased and waxpaper-lined 9x5x3 loaf pan. Bake at 350F for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Cool 10 minutes before turning out of pan.</br>
