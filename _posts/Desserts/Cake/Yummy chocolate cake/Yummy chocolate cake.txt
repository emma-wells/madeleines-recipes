#Yummy chocolate cake
##From Grandma
<br>Bring to boil 1 stick Oleo, 1/2 cup oil, 4 tablespoons cocoa, 1 cup water.</br>
<br>In a small bowl, beat 2 eggs, add 1/2 cup sour milk, 1 teaspoon baking soda, 1 teaspoon vanilla.</br>
<br>In a large bowl, put 2 cups flour and 2 cups sugar. Pour chocolate sauce over the flour and sugar. Mix in the egg mixture. Mix well. Put in a 15 1/2" 10 1/2" pan. Bake at 350F about 25-30 minutes.</br>




<br>Emma's note: Oleo is a kind of margarine, according to Google.</br>
