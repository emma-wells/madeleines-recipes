#Wowie cake
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 1/2 cups flour
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1/4 cup cocoa (unsweetened)
* 1 teaspoon soda
* 1 tablespoon vinegar
* 1/3 cup oil 
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 1 cup water

<br>Sift first five ingredients into ungreased 8x8" pan. Add remaining ingredients and stir. Bake at 350F for 35 minutes. Can double into 9x13" pan.</br>
