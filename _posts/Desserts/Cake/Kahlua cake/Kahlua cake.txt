#Kahlua cake
##From Grandma
###Bake 350F - 45 minutes
###Ingredients
* 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
* 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
* 2 cups sifted flour
* 3/4 teaspoon salt
* 1/2 teaspoon mace
* 1/3 cup chopped nuts
* 1 teaspoon baking powder
* 3/4 teaspoon baking soda

<br>In bowl combine:</br>
* 1 1/4 cup sugar
* 1/2 cup butter
* 2 eggs
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
<br> Beat 2 minutes. Blend alternately with flour with 3/4 sour cream and 1/3 cup kahlua.</br>
<br>Turn 1/3 of batter into greased and floured 9" tube pan. Cover with filling, then another 1/3 batter, then remaining filling. Top with remaining batter.</br>
