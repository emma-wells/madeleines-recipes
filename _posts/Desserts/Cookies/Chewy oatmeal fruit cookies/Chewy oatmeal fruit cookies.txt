#Chewy oatmeal fruit cookies
##From Nana's (?)
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 1 cup shortening
<br>Cream together and add:</br>

* 2 large eggs
* 1 teaspoon vanilla

<br>Sift together:</br>

* 1 cup flour
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspoon baking soda

<br>And add to the above. Then add:</br>

* 4 cups oatmeal
* 1 cup raisins
* 1 cup dates
* 1 cup nuts
* 1 cup coconut

<br>Drop by teaspoons onto a greased cookie sheet. Makes 5 dozen cookies.</br>

<br>Writer's note: Sometimes I use 3 eggs.</br>
