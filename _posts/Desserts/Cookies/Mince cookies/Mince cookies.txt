#Mince cookies
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 3 1/4 cup sifted flour
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1 package fruit mince meat
* 1 cup shortening
* 1 1/2 cups sugar
* 3 eggs well beaten

<br> Sift together flour, salt and baking soda. Cream shortening and sugar gradually; add eggs. Beat until smooth and add mince meat broken into small pieces, along with flour mixture, and mix well.</br><br>Drop by teaspoons onto a greased cookie sheet and bake at 400F for 12 minutes.</br>
