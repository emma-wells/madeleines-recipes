#Coffee walnut coconut drop
##From Grandma
<br>Beat 1 whole egg and 1 egg yolk until light. Beat in 3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar. Gradually beat in 1 tablespoon flour, 1 tablespoon instant coffee powder, 2 teaspoons soft butter.</br><br>Add 2 cups of coarsely broken walnuts, 1 1/2 cups flaked coconut, stirring until well blended.</br><br>Drop by rounded spoonfuls on a greased cookie sheet. Bake at 300F for 12-14 minutes. Leave on sheets 3 minutes, then carevule move to wire racks to cool.</br>
