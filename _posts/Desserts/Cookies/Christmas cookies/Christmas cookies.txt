#Christmas cookies
##From Grandma
<br>Melt together a small package of chocolate chips (or 4 squares of chocolate) and 4 tablespoons of butter. Cool chocolate and add 2 slightly beaten eggs. Then add:</br>

* 1 cup crushed Arrowroot cookies
* 1 cup ground walnuts
* 1 cup confectionary sugar
* 1 package minuature colored marshmallows

<br>Roll mixture into 4 rolls, each the diameter of a $0.50 piece. Put on wax paper and roll in coconut. Then chill and later cut into approximately 1/4 inch slices.</br>
