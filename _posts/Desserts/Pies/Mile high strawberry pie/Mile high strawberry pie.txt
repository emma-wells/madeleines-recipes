#Mile high strawberry pie
##From Grandma
* 1 1/4 cup or 10 oz thawed frozen berries
* 1 cup sugar
* 2 egg whites
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1 tablesooon lemon juice
<br>Mix together in a large bowl. Beat at high speed until very stiff and holds shape (15 minutes). </br>
<br>Beat 1/2 cup cream until stiff. Add 1 teaspoon vanilla</br>
<br> Fold into fruit mixture and pileinto a cooled pastry shell. Freeze several hours, or overnight. Serve directly from the freezer. Will keep 2 weeks frozen.</br>
