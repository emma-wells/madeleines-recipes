#Kentucky pecan pie
##From Mom and Dear Abby
###Ingredients
* 1 cup white corn syrup
* 1 cup dark brown sugar
* 1/3 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 3 eggs, slightly beaten
* 1/3 cup butter 
* 1 heaping cup shelled whole pecans
<br>Combine syrup, sugar, butter, vanilla and mix well. Add slightly beaten eggs. Pour in a 9 inch unbaked pie shell. Sprinkle pecans over all. Bake in a preheated oven at 350F for 45 minutes.
