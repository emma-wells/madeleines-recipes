#Impossible pie
##From Grandma, Ida 1987
###Ingredients
* 1/2 cup biscuit mix
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 4 eggs
* 2 cups milk
* 1 1/2 cup coconut
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 3 tablespoons butter
<br>Put all incredients in mixer or blender. When well blended, pour into a buttered 10 inch pie plate. Bake for 350F, 30-35 minutes.</br>
