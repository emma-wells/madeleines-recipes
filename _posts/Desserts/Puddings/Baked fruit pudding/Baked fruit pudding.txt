#Baked fruit pudding
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 cup flour
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 2 cups fruit cocktail
* 1 teaspoon cinnamon
* 1 egg

<br>Sift dry ingredients, egg, and fruit cocktail. Mix all together and pur into a buttered 9" square pan.</br><br>Sprinkle top with brown sugar and nuts. Bake 325F for 40-50 minutes.</br>
