#Carrot, pineapple, nut bread
##From Grandma
* 3 eggs, beaten
* 2 cups sugar
* 1 cup Wessen (canola) oil
* 2 cups grated carrots (not packed)
* 1 small can crushed pineapple (use all)
* 2 teaspoons vanilla
* 3 cups flour
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspoon cinnamon
* 1 cup nuts
<br>Bake 350F, about 1 hour.</br>
