## From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 package pasta
* 1/4 cup unsalted butter
* 3/4 cup heavy cream
* 1/4 pound Gorgonzola dolce cheese


Make sauce an pour over pasta. Then sprinkle with walnuts and nutmeg.
