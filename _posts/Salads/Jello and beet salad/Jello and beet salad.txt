#Jello and beet salad
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 can drained julienne beets
* 1 small can cdrained crushed pineapple

<br>To the juice of the pineapple and beets, add 1/4 cup vinegar, 1 tablesppon sugar, and the juice of 1 lemon. Dissolve 1 package of rasberry jello with the juices. Chill and serve garnished with mayonnaise or sour cream.</br>
