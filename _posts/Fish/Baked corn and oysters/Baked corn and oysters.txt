#Baked corn and oysters
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 17-ounce cream-style corn
* 1 cup crackers, crushed
* 1 beaten egg
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1/2 cup milk
* 1/4 teaspoon pepper
* 1 teaspoon sugar
* 1/2 cup melted butter
* 1 cup fresh oysters


<br>Combine ingredients in the order named, and pour the mixture into a greased 1 1/2 quart baking dish. Bake at 375F for 25 minutes.</br>
