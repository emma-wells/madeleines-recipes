#Fried crab cakes
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 pound fresh crabmeat
* 2 sliced old bread, crumbled
* 1 teaspoon mustard
* 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
* salt, pepper
* Lots of red pepper
* 1 tablespoon parsley

<br>Mix together lightly, and form into 8 cakes; put in the refridgerator for a day. Fry slowly in butter.</br>
