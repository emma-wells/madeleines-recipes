## From Mom
###Pastry crust
* 1 3/4 cup flour
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1/2 cup oil
* 3 tablespoons water
###Cherry pie
* 1 cup cherry pie filling
* 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
* 1/8 teaspoon almond flavouring
