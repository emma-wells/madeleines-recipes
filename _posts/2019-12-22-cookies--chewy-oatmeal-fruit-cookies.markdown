## From Nana's (?)
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 1 cup shortening

Cream together and add:


* 2 large eggs
* 1 teaspoon vanilla


Sift together:


* 1 cup flour
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspoon baking soda


And add to the above. Then add:


* 4 cups oatmeal
* 1 cup raisins
* 1 cup dates
* 1 cup nuts
* 1 cup coconut


Drop by teaspoons onto a greased cookie sheet. Makes 5 dozen cookies.



Writer's note: Sometimes I use 3 eggs.
