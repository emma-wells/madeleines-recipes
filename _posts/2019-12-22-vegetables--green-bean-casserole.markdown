## From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 cup mushroom soup
* 3/4 cup milk
* 1/8 teaspoon pepper
* 1 1/2 cups french onions
* 2 cans green beans


Mix soup and milk, then add pepper, green beans, and 2/3 onions into a 1 1/2 quart casserole. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes. Stir in remaining onions and bake for 5 more minutes.
