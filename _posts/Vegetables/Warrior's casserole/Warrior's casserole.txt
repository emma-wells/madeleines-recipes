#Warrior's casserole
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 pound ground beef
* 1 cup cream of chicken soup
* 4 teaspoons green chillies
* 1 package corn tortillas
* 1 stick (10 ounces) cheddar cheese
* 1 cup milk
* 1 teaspoon garlic salt

<br>Brown meat; drain and slice a 2 inch chunk from the cheese. Cut into 6 arrows 2 1/4 inches thick for garnish. Shred remaining cheese. Combine meat, shredded cheese, soup, milk, chillies, garlic. In a 1 1/2 or 2-quart serving casserole, layer 1 cup of meat mixture and 4 tortillas. Repeat layers 2 more times and top with remaining meat mixture.</br>
