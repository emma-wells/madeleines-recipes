#Potato casserole
##From ??
###Ingredients
* 1 2-pound package of hash brown frozen potatoes
* 1 1/2 sticks Oleo (shortening)
* 1 cup chopped onions
* 1 package shredded cheddar cheese
* 1/2 can chicken soup
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspon garlic salt 
* 1 pint sour cream
* cornflakes

<br>Defrost potatoes. Reserve 1/2 stick Oleo and 2 cups of corn flakes. Mix everything and put in the oven in a 9x13 inch pan. Mix reserved Oleo and cornflakes. and sprinkle onto the top. Bake 350F 1-1 1/2 hours.</br>
