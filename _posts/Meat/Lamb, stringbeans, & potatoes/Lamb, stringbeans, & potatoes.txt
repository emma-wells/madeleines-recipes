#Lamb, stringbeans, & potatoes
##From Grandma
<br>Cover bottom of a pressure cooker with olive oil. Cut up 1 small onion and fry. Salt and pepper meat. Brown meat in this also. Dilute 2 tablespoons of tomato paste in 1 cup water and add to the mix. Add string beans and cut potatoes. Add salt, pepper and oregano.</br><br> Set pressure cooker at 10 pounds and cook 20 minutes after the jiggle begins.</br>
