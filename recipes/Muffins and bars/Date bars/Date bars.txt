#Date bars
##From Ellie DiVirgilio
###Ingredients
* 1 cup chopped dates
* 1 cup chopped nuts
* 1 cup sifted flour
* 3 eggs
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1 teaspoon baking powder
* 1/2 teaspoon salt

<br>1. Mix dates and nuts with flour</br><br>2. In a separate bowl, beat eggs well and add sugar. Add this to step #1.</br><br>3. Add other ingredients and blend thoroughly. Put in a 9x13-inch pan. Bake at 350F for 20 to 25 minutes.</br>
