#Czechoslovakian bars
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 cup butter (2 sticks)
* 1 cup sugar
* 2 cups flour
* 2 egg yolks
* 1/8 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 1 cup walnuts
* Rasberry jam

<br>Cream butter and sugar and add egg yolks. Add other ingredients and beat (will be very stiff) and add walnuts last.</br><br>Place 1/2 of batter into a buttered pan - spread with jam. Add other half of batter. Place in a cold oven and bring to 325F for 1 hour.</br><br>Can be varied with apricot or peach preserve.</br>
