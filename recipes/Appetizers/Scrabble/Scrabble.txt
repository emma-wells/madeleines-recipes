#Scrabble
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 3-4 cans mixed nuts
* 6 ounces Very Thin Pretzel
* 1 package 10.5-ounce Cheerios
* 1 package 12-ounce Wheat Chex
* 1 package 8.5-ounce Rice Chex

<br>Blend together:</br>

* 2 1/2 cups peanut oil
* 2 1/2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
* 1 1/2 teaspoons garlic salt
* 1 1/2 teaspoons celery salt

<br>Stir into oil mixture into ingredients very carefully. Bake in a 250F oven for 1 hour, and stir every 15 minutes.</br>
