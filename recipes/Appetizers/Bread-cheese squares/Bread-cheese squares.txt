#Bread-cheese squares
##From ??
* 1 loaf thin bread
* 1 jar of Kraft Old English Cheese, softened
* 1/4 pound butter (1 stick), softened

<br>Mix:</br>
* 4 strips bacon, cooked
* 1 onion, chopped
* 1 teaspoon horseradish sauce
* dash tobasco

<br>Cut crust off bread. Spread mixture on three slices of bread and cut into fourths. Broil about 5 minutes until very golden.</br>
