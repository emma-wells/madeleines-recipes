#Bacon tomato cups
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 8 slices bacon, crisply cooked
* 1 medium tomato
* 1/2 small onion
* 3 ounces swiss cheese
* 1/2 cup mayonnaise
* 1 teaspoon basil
* 1 can (10 ounces) refrigerated flaky biscuits

<br>Mix all incredients except biscuits and set aside. Separate each biscuit inot 3 thinner biscuits. Lay each biscuit over miniature muffins. Mix remainder. Bake 375F for 12-15 minutes.
