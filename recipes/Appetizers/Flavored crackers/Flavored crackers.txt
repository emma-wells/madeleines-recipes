#Flavoured crackers
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 12 ounces oyster crackers
<br>Mix 2/3 cup oil in a 13x9" pan. Then blend together:</br>
* 1 package Hidden Valley Ranch Original dressing (dry package)
* 1/2 teaspoon lemon pepper
* 1/2 teaspoon dill
* 1/2 teaspoon onion powder

<br>Sprinkle through strainer over crackers. Heat oven to 300F and turn off. Leave pan in oven for 1/2 hour.</br>
