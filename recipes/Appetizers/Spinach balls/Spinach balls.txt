#Spinach balls
##From Helen Kyslowsky
###Ingredients
* 2 cups Pepperidge Farm stuffinf
* 4 eggs, beaten
* 2 packages of chopped, frozen spinach (drained)
* 1 small chopped onion
* 3/4 cup butter, melted
* 1 1/2 teaspoons thyme
* 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, or any cheese
* dash pepper

<br> Mix together. Let stand overnight. Shape into balls the next day. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet for 20 minutes - 350F or until brown.</br>
<br>Good luck</br>
