#Bow tie macaroni
##From Grandma
<br>Cook 3 cups of bowties in boiling, salted water until barely tender. Meanwhile prepare sauce. Saute 2 tablespoons of chopped onion and 2 tablespoons of chopped green pepper lightly in 2 tablesppons of butter in a skillet.</br><br>Blend in 1 1/2 tablespoons of flour, 3/4 teaspoon salt. Stir in 1 cup milk until thick. Stir in 1/4 cup Sauterne or other white dinner wine, 1 1/2 cups grated cheddar cheese, and heat slowly until the cheese melts.</br><bt>Drain macaroni, then add to sauce and mix lightky. Heat slowly for a few minutes. Makes 4 generous servings.</br>
