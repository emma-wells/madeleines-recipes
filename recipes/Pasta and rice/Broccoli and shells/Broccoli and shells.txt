#Broccoli and shells
##From Mom
###Ingredients
* 1 medium-size bunch of fresh broccoli
* 4 cups shells
* 3 tablespoons butter
* 3/4 cup heavy cream
* 2/3 cup grated parmesan
* water
* salt

<br>1. Wash and cut the broccoli into 1/2 inch pieces, making about 4 cups.</br><br>2. Cook the pasta for 3 minutes in boiling water, add broccoli, and boil until both are tender.</br><br>4. Drain pasta and broccoli</br><br>5. Toss all ingredients in a large bowl until butter melts. Add the cheese now.</br>
