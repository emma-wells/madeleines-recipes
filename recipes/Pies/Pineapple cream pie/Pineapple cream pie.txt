#Pineapple cream pie
##From Grandma
###Makes 6 serviings
* 3 tablespoons sugar
* 2 tablespoons cornstarch (cornflour)
* 1 can (20 oz) pineapple chunks
* 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
* 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
* 1 cup dairy sour cream
* Whipped cream for garnish, cherries or coconut
* Premade graham cracker crust
<br>For filling, blend sugar and cornstarch in a saucepan; gradually stir in syrup drawined from pineapple chunks. Cook until thickened and clear, stirring. Remove from heat. Stir in vanilla, almond extract and sour cream. Gently fold filling into pie shell. Chill 1-2 hours before serving. Garnish with whipped cream and cherries on coconut.</br>
