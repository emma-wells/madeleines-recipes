#Custard pie
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 2 large eggs
* 3/4 sugar
* 3 cups milk, boiling hot
* 1 rounded tablespoon flour
* 1/2 teaspoon salt

<br>Beat eggs and sugar, flour and salt. Pour over the mixture the boiling milk and stir. Pour in an unbaked pie - bake 15 minutes for 400F then 325F for 15 minutes. Sprinkle with nutmeg. </br>
