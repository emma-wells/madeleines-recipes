#Treasure cake
##From Mom, Spry co.
###Ingredients
* 2 cups sifted cake flour
* 1 1/3 cup sugar
* 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1/2 cup spry
* 1 cup less 2 tablespoons milk
* 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
* 2 eggs
<br>Sift first 4 ingredients into mixing bowl. Drop in Spry. Add about 2/3 cup milk, then vanilaa and beat 200 strokes. Scrape bowl and spoon. Add remaining eggs and milk. Grease 2 pans and bake at 375F, 25-35 minutes.
