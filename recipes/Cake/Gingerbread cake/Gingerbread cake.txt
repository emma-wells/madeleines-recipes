#Gingerbread cake
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 3 cups sifted flour
* 1 teaspoon cinnamon
* 1 teaspoon ginger
* 1 teaspoon cloves
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 1/2 cup melted shortening
* 1 cup molasses
* 2 eggs
* 1 cup boiling water

<br>Sift flour, cinnamon, ginger, cloves and baking soda together and set aside.</br>
<br>Stir brown sugar into melted shortening. Add eggs and molasses and beat well (use wooden spoon).</br>
<br>Add hot water alternating with dry ingredients.</br>
<br>Bake in 350F oven 40-50 minutes.</br>
