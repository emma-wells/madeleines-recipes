#Lemon torte
##From Grandma
###Mix:
* 1 cup flour
* 1/2 cup Oleo
* 1/4 cup chopped nuts
<br>Spread in a glass 7x11" baking dish. Bake at 375F for 15 minutes.</br>
###Mix:
* 8 ounce cream cheese
* 1 cup confectionary sugar
* 1/2 large Cool Whip

<br>Spread over cooled crust.</br>

###Beat:
* 2 packages instant lemon pudding
* 3 cups milk
* 1/2 package Cool Whip

<br>Beat until stiff - spread on top of cream cheese mixture. Top with the rest of the Cool Whip. Sprinkle top with 1/4 cup chopped walnut. Refridgerate.</br>
