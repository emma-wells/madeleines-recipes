#Streusel coffee cake
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1/3 cup Crisco oil
* 1 egg
* 1/2 cup milk
* 1 1/2 cups sifted regular flour
* 2 teaspoons double-acting baking powder
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
<br>Combine sugar, Crisco Oil and egg. Add milk and beat thoroughly. Stir in combined dry ingredients. Beat until smooth. Spread in a greased 9-inch square pan. Sprinkle with streusel topping. Bake at 375F for 30-35 minutes.</br>
###Streusal topping
* 1/2 cup light brown sugar
* 2 tablespoons regular flour
* 2 teaspoons cinnamon
* 2 tablespoons Crisco Oil
* 1/2 cup finely chopped walnuts

<br>Combine all ingredients with a fork. Sprinkle on batter.</br>
