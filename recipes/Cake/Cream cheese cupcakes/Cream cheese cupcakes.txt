#Cream cheese cupcakes
##From ??
<br>Place vanilla cookie in foil cups (take out liner). Add a mixture of:</br>

* 2 large cream cheese
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 2 eggs
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
<br>Mix well. Bake 10 minutes at 375F. Make 24. Top with pie filling (pineapple or cherry).</br>
