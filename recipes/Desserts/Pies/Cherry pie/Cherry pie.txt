#Cherry pie
##From Grandma
<br>A. 1 1/2 cup graham crackers, 3/4 stick margerine melted. Bake at 325F for 10 minutes.</br>
<br>B. 1/2 cup confectionary sugar, 2 eggs  whole, 1 stick margerine. Beat 5 minutes, medium speed.</br>
<br>C. Let pie crust cool before filling. Then put in sugar mixture which is very stiff.</br>
<br> D. Put in cherry pie filling</br>
<br>Let sit for 3 hours or overnight in fridge.</br>
