#Brown bettys
##From Grandma via Irene Comeau 
###Ingredients
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 3/4 cup melted shortening
* 2 eggs
* 2 1/2 cups flour
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 1 cup currants
* 1/2 cup walnuts
* 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
* 2 tablespoons milk

<br>Put in refridgerator to cool, then drop by spoonful onto a cookie sheet and bake 300-350F.</br>
