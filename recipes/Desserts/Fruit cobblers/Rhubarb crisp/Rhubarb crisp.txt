#Rhubarb crisp
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 2 cups rhubarb
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1/2 cup flour
* Lemon juice

<br>Toss rubarb with sugar, lemon juice and flour. Transfer mix to a baking dish.</br>
###Topping
* 1 cup flour
* 1/2 cup granulated sugar
* 3/4 cup rolled oats
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 1 teaspoon cinnamon
* 12 tablespooons butter (1 1/2 sticks)

<br>Combine with a pastry blender until it resembles crumbs. Spread over fruit. Bake for 50 minutes at 350F in a 9x13" pan.</br>
