#Jelly roll
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 3 eggs
* 3 tablespoons cold water
* 1 cup flour
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 teaspoon baking powder
* Jam 

<br>Beat eggs lightly, add remaining incredients one at a time, and continue beating. Bake at 350F for 10 minutes. Turn out cake onto a dish towel sprinkled with confectionary suger. Spread quickly with jam and roll with towel.
