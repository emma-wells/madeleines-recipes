#Fruit cake
##From Mom, via Fireman's Wife
###Ingredients
* 2 cups flour
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 2 teaspoons baking powder
* 1/2 cup butter
* 1 cup sugar
* 3 eggs
* 1/4 cup orange juice
* 1 teaspoon lemon extract
* 1 large size jar of candied fruits

<br>Combine as for a simple cake, adding the fruit last. It is not necessary to flour the fruit. Pour into 1 pound coffee cans and bake at 325F for 1 1/2 hours. Nuts may be added but no raisins. Age it.</br>
