#Molasses cookies
##From Grandma
<br>Cream together:</br>
* 1/2 cup shortening
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1/4 cup molasses
* 1 egg
<br>Sift together:</b>
* 1 1/2 cup flour
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/2 teaspoon salt

<br>Add 1/2 cup coconut <u>or</u> walnuts or raisins. Drop on sheet by teaspoonful. Cook 10 minutes in a moderate oven at 375F.</br>

<br>Grandma's note: Double this!</br>
