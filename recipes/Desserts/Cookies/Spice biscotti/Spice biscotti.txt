#Spice biscotti
##From ??
## 23 February 1985
###Ingredients
* 3 eggs
* 1/2 cup oil
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1/2 cup orange juice
* 1/2 cup grated orange rind
* 1 teaspoon allspice
* 1/2 teaspoon cloves
* 4 cups flour (about)
* 1 teaspoon cinnamon
* 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
* 1 teaspoon baking powder
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/2 cup raisins
* 1/2 cup chopped nuts
* 1/2 cup chopped maraschino cherries
<br>Beat eggs, add oil, sugar, juice, allspice, cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg, baking powder, and baking soda.</br>
<br>Mix well.</br> 
<br>Add raisins, cherries, nuts and rind, and mix. Add enough flour to keep dough soft. Form long strips on cookie sheet.</br>
<br>Bake at 375F for about 15 minutes or until done.
