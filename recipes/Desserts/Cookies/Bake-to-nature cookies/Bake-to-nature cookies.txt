#Bake-to-nature cookies
##From Mom
###Ingredients
* 2 cups sifted all-purpose flour
* 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspoon cinnamon
* 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
* 2/3 cups soft shortening
* 2/3 cup firmly packed brown sugar
* 1 egg
* 1/4 cup honey
* 1/2 cup mashed ripe banana
* 2 cxups 100% natural cereal
<br>Mix and sift flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a large bowl. Add shortening, brown sugar, egg, honey and banna. Beat together until thoroughly combined. Stir in cereal. Cover and chill dough 1 hour. </br>
<br>Drop dough by teaspoonfuls onto greased cookie sheets. Bake at 350F for 12-15 minutes. Cool on wire rack. Makes 5 dozen. </br>
###Honey butter frosting
* 1/4 cup butter
* 2 cups sifted confectioners sugar
* 1/4 cup honey
* 1 teaspoon water
<br>Beat together honey and butter. Gradually beat in confectionars sugar and water. Frost cooled cookies.</br>
