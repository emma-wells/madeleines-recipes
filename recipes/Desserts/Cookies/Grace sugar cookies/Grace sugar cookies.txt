#Grace sugar cookies
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 2/3 cup butter
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 4 teaspoons milk
* 1 1/2 teaspoons baking poqder
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 1 egg
* 2 cups flour
* 3/4 cup sugar

<br>Divide dough in half. Cover and chill for at least one hour.</br>
<br>On a lightly floured surgface, roll to 1/8" thickness. Cut into desired shape and put on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 375F for 8-10 minutes.</br>
