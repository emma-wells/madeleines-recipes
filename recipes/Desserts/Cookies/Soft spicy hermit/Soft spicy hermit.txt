#Soft spicy hermit
##From Grandma
###Method
1. Cream 1 cup of sugar and 1/2 cup salad oil.
2. Sift together:
	1. 3 cups flour
	2. 1 teaspoon cinnamon
	3. 1/2 teaspoon salt
	4. 1 teaspoon baking soda


<br>Add this mix alternately with 1/2 cup milk and 1/2 cup molasses. Finally add 3/4 cup raisins.</br>
<br> Spread on cookie sheet and bake at 350F for 20-25 minutes.</br>
<br>Cut into squares and sprinkle sugar on top when done.</br>
