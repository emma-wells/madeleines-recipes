#Lemon cookies
##From ??
###Ingredients
* 1/2 cup oil and 1/4 cup Crisco
* 1 cup sugar
* 4 eggs
* 2 tablespoons lemon flavoring
* 4 cups flour
* 2 tablespoons baking powder

<br>Sift tgoether flour and baking powder; set aside. Mix well the Crisco and oil with sugar, eggs and lemon flavoring.</br><br>Add flour to wet ingredients.</br><br>If dough seems dry, add a little milk about 1/4 cup at a time. Sometimes you don't need milk, depending on the size of the eggs.</br><br>Make small balls using a small cookie scoop.</br><br>Bake at 350F for 10-12 minutes. Bottoms should be light brown.</br>
