#Nieman Marcus cookies
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 2 cups butter
* 4 cups flour
* 2 teaspoons soda
* 2 cups sugar
* 5 cups blend oatmeal
* 2 teaspoons baking powder
* 3 cups chopped nuts
* 24 ounces chocolate chips
* 2 cups brown sugar
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1 8-ounce Henry Bar (grated)
* 4 eggs
* 2 teaspoons vanilla

<br>Measure oatmeal and blend in a blender into a fine powder. Cream butter and both sugars, eggs, and vanilla. Mix together with flour, oatmeal, salt, baking powder, baking soda, chips and bar, nuts.</br>
<br>Roll into balls and place 2 inches apart on a cookie sheet. Bake 375F for 10 minutes. Makes 112 cookies.</br>
<br>Emma's note: 112 cookies! 112!</br>
