#Crabmeat and rice au gratin
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 1 7-ounce can crabmeat
* 2 cup cooked rice
* 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
* 2 cups hot medium white sauce
* 1 teaspoon onion
* salt and pepper
* 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
* Paprika

<br>Flake crabmeat. Remove hard membrane. Arrange alternate layers of crabmeat and rice. Melt cheese in hot white sauce seasoned with onion, salt and pepper; pour over contents of casserole. Top with crumbs, sprinkle with paprika. Bake in moderate oven of 350F for 30 minutes. Serves 4</br>
