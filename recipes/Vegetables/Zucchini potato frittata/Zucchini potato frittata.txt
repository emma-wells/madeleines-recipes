#Zucchini potato frittata
##From Mom
###Ingredients
* 1 cup raw, peeled, grated zucchini
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 2 eggs, separated
* 2 medium potatoes, mashed
* 1/2 onion sauteed
* 2 ounces parmesan cheese
* garlic powder
* salt
* pepper

<br>Sprinkle zucchini with salt and let stand ten minutes. Drain. Combine zucchini with lightly beaten egg yolks. Add mashed potato, onion, half of cheese, and spices. Beat egg whites with a pinch of salt until stiff. Fold into zucchini. Pour into a small casserole dish. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top. Bake at 350F for 30-40 minutes. Serve hot.</br>
