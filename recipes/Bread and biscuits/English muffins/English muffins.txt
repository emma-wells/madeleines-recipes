#English muffins
##From Mom
<br>Mix these:</br>
* 2 cups flour
* 2 tablespoons sugar
* 2 teaspoons salt
* 1 tablespoon yeast

<br>Heat until warm:</br>
* 1 2/3 cups milk
* 1/4 cup water
* 1 tablespoon butter

<br>Mix the dry ingredients with the warm liquid. Add:</br>
* 1 egg
* 4 cups flour

<br>Add egg and enough flour to make a soft dough. Knead. Cover, and rise about 1 hour. Punch. Rise again until doubled, about 45 minutes. Punch. </br><br>On a floured board, roll until 1/2 inch thick. With a 3 1/4 inch cutter, cut out. Place on cookie sheets sprinkled with 1/2 cup cornmeal. Cover. Let rise until double, about 45 minutes. Heat a lightly greased griddle. Cook over low heat, 8-10 minutes each side. Cool. Makes 24.</br>
###Variations
* Onion: Add 1 envelope onion soup mix and 3 tablespoons nonfat dry milk to the dry ingredients. Decrease salt. Increase butter. Use 2 cups of water.
* Mushroom: Decrease salt and add 1/2 teaspoon thyme. No milk + 100g can mushroom soup plus milk to make 1 3/4 cups. Add 4 ounces mushrooms chopped.
* Cheese: Add 2 cups of cheddar with egg. Good for hamburgers.
* Maple walnut: USe brown sugar or molasses. Add 1/2 teaspoon maple extract and 1 cup finely chopped walnuts.
* Other: Chopped fruit, mashed sweet potato, orange peel, whole wheat, oatmeal, cornmeal, rye, wheatgerm.
