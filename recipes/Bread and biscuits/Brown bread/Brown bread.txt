#Brown bread
##From Mom via Farm Journal
###Ingredients
* 1 1/2 cups sifted flour
* 1 1/2 cups rye flour
* 1 cup cornmeal
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1/2 cup raisins (Mom's note:increase)
* 2/3 cups walnuts (Mom's note: omit)
* 2 cups buttermilk
* 1/2 cup dark molasses

<br>Stir dry ingredients, add raisins, slowly add molasses and milk, alternately. Beat. </br><br>Pour batter into a loaf pan and bake at 375F for 50 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes in pan. </br>
