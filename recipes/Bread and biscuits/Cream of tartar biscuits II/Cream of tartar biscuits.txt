#Cream of tartar biscuits
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 3 cups flour
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 2 teaspoon cream of tartar
* 1 1/2 tablespoon shortening
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1 cup milk

<br>Mix and sift flour, salt, cream of tartar. Work in shortening with fingertips. Mix in the baking soda, milk and flour mixture.</br><br>Mix thoroughly and knead on a flour board until pastry. Roll out to 3/4-inch thickness. Put in a greased pan, with space between them. Bake at 425F for 15 minutes.</br>
