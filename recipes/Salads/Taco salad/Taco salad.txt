#Taco salad
##From ??
###Ingredients
* 1 large head lettuce
* 1 large, fresh, ripe tomato
* 1 large red onion
* 1 1/2 -2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
* 1 pound ground beef or chuck, browned
* 1 8-ounce bottle of Ranch dressing

<br>Cut lettuce and tomato; trim as you would for a tossed salad. Put in a large salad bowl and add cheese and chips.</br>
