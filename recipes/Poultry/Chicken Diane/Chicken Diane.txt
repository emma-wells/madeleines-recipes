#Chicken Diane
##From ??
<br> 2 1/2 pounds chicken: boil with an onion. Skin and cut up. Put in a 9x3 inch pan and make a roux: 6 tablespoons margarinem 1/2 cup flour. Add 1 cup of milk, 1 can of celery soup, and 1 can of chicken broth. Cook until thickened. Pour this gravy over the chicken. Mix 1 small package of Pepperidge Farm stuffing and 1 stick of melted butter together. Put on top of the casserole. Bake at 350F for 45 minutes - 1 hour.</br>
