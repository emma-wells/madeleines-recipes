#Crystal Hobmail's fried chicken
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 3 pounds of chicken, cut up
* 1/4 cup water
* 1/4 cup lemon juice or vinegar
* 1 clove garlic, chopped
* 1/2 cup flour
* 2 teaspoons paprika
* 2 teaspoons dried mustard
* 1 tablespoon salt
* 1/8 teaspoon salt

<br>Let the chicken stand one hour in the water, vinegar, and garlic.</br><br>Flour them and then pan fry. Place in a 350F oven for 30 minutes.</br>
