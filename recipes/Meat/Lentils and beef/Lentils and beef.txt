#Lentils and beef
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 2 cups washed lentils
* 1 pound ground beef
* 1 cup catsup or tomato sauce
* 1 package onion soup mix
* 1 teaspoon cider vinegar
* 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
* 1 quart water
* 1/2 cup salad oil
* 1/8 teaspoon pepper
* 1 cup water

<br>Combine lentils and water in large saucepan. Cook uncovered over medium heat for 30 minutes. Brown beef in oil. Stir in drained lentils and remianing ingredients. Pour into a 2 1/2 quart casserole dish. Bake uncovered at 400F for 30 minutes.</br>
