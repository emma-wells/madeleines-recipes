#Corned beef stuffed baked potatoes
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 4 large baking potatoes
* 2/4 cup margarine
* 1/3 cup milk
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* pepper
* 1 cup cubed, cooked corned beef
* 1/4 cup sour cream
* 3/4 teaspoon horseradish
* chopped parsley

<br>A. Preheat oven to 425F. Bake potatoes 50-60 minutes or until easily pricked by a fork.</br><br>B. Cool slightly, then slash tops. Scoop out potato; keep skins intact.</br><br>C. Combine sour cream and horseradish, and spoon over potatoes.</br>
