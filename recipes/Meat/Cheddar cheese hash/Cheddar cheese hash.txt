#Cheddar cheese hash
##From Grandma
###Ingredients
* 4 cups diced, cold boiled potato
* 1 chopped onion
* 3 tablespoons flour
* salt
* pepper
* 1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
* 1/4 cup milk
* 1/2 pound thinly sliced hotdogs
* 3 tablespoons fat

<br>Combine potato and onion. Sprinkle with flour, salt and pepper. Add milk and frankfurters. Put in a greased, shallow baking dish or pie pan. Dot with fat. Bake at 425F for 30 minutes. Top with cheese bake for 5 minutes longer, or until cheese is melted.</br>
